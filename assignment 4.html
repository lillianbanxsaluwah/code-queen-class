GAPS MODEL OF SERVICE QUALITY

GAP1 KNOWLEDGE GAP:Difference between the customer's expectections of the service and what the manager thinks they expect and need.
GAP2 THE POLITICAL GAP:Difference between manager understanding of the custmer's needs and the translating of that understanding in to service delivery and standards.
GAP3 DELIEVERY GAP:Difference between service delivery policy and standards and the actual delivery of services.
GAP4 COMMIUNICATION GAP:Difference between what gets promised to customers through advertising and what gets delivered.
GAP5 CUSTOMERS GAP:Difference between customers expectations and customers things delivered.
